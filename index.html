ABOUT US

Naph's Pizzeria was established in the year 2019.

We use only the highest-quality ingredients, including cow-milk mozzarella tomatoes imported from Australia as well as our fresh, home-made mozzarella.

All pizzas are drizzled with 100% Italian extra-virgin olive oil and then baked to perfection at 850 degrees in a Mario Acunto wood-fired brick oven from Naples.

Our customers have always been happy on the delivery services we offer and the ingredients we use to make our pizza.
